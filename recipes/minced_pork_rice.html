Minced Port Rice 滷肉飯

Description

Here's the recipe on how to make a perfect Taiwanese Minced Port Rice

Ingredients

  • 500 g diced pork belly (or minced)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion(already fried is okay too)
  • 1/2 cup soya sauce
  • 30 g oyster sauce
  • 1/2 cup white rice wine
  • 20 g rock sugar
  • 2 star anise or 1 teaspoon of five spice powder
  • A few dashes of white pepper
  • enough water to cover (500~700ml)
  • some boiled eggs - optional
  • some chopped shallot
